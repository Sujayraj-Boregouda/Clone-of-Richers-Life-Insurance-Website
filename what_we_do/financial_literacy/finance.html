Financial Literacy

“Children must be taught how to think, not what to think.” ― Margaret Mead

The Richer Life – wellness initiative is established to help our clients take educated and informed decisions with their money, so that there are no regrets tomorrow. We bring experienced Financial Advisors together with budding clients who want to learn the financial lingo so they can be guided to make the right decisions.

We conduct workshops for our clients, corporates, investors where we discuss personal financial matters that help them understand the world of finance. Our sessions can prepare people to confidently deal with their own money and increase their productivity. We train advisors on guiding individuals to invest.

We work closely with schools and colleges to teach good money habits to children. We also run programs for students seeking a career in the line of personal finance.
