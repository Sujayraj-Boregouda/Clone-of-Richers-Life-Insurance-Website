About Us

Richer Life is a leading name in Financial advisory located in Bangalore. Our sole aim is to provide you an end to end wealth management solutions under one roof.

From Investment advisory, financial planning to insurance and real estate, Richer Life will take care of all your financial needs with great precision and care. Our contrarian approach coupled with a single focus on long term investments will ensure customers are provided with the most tax efficient and the best risk adjusted returns. Come join our family and enjoy eternal financial freedom.

Meet Our Team


Jigisha Rajguru

With a Masters in commerce from Mumbai University, Jigisha come with a rich experience in accounts, Taxation and personal finances over a period of 18 years. She has worked With the Big 4 accounting Companies like PWC in the space of taxation for Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs), Expats, and Individuals and has also handled various Responsibilities in auditing and company registration.

During the past 9 years, Jigisha has had her own business in the personal finance domain, helping People with insurance and investment plans, Financial, Real Estate and other structured Products.

Networker at heart, Jigisha passionately believes in investor education and financial independenee for women and want to go the extra mile with implementing the idea of financial literacy even for school and college kids.
